Educational and Personal History

My name is Danielle Schieber. I am currently pursuing a Master’s degree in Library and Information Science (MLIS) from the iSchool at University of Missouri. I earned my M.A. in English from Truman State University in 2012. I instructed an introductory course on Writing as Critical Thinking while in graduate school. I received my B.A. from the same university in 2009.
I served as a Cryptologic Technician Interpretive in the US Navy. In 2015, I received my A.A. in Arabic from the Defense Language Institute in Monterey, California. I spent the last four years of my Navy service in Augusta, Georgia. I hope to work either in a public or special library in the near future and have potential plans to pursue a PhD. In my spare time, I crochet, study languages, and read.
